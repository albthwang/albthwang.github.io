Math Tutoring

Focused support that strengthens foundations, builds confidence, and helps learners excel through complex topics.

Why math can be challenging

Math builds on itself. Each new topic depends on the ones before it, so a small gap from an earlier year can quietly hold a learner back later. My work focuses on understanding core concepts, strengthening problem-solving, and rebuilding the confidence that gaps tend to erode.

Is your learner a good fit?

I work with two kinds of learners, and often the same learner moves between them over time.

Strengthening foundations

For a learner who has fallen behind or feels lost, we reinforce key concepts and fill in any gaps so the current coursework finally makes sense.

Accelerating and excelling

For a learner who is ready for more, we use tailored sessions that challenge them, spanning topics from 6th grade math through AP Calculus and SAT prep.

What parents can expect

Steady, structured progress and honest communication about how things are going. The goal is not just a better grade on the next test. It is a learner who understands the material and trusts themselves to work through it.
