Meet Albert

A guide who takes the work seriously

Albert is a professional tutor with over 15 years of experience supporting middle and high school learners in Mathematics, Mandarin Chinese, and long-term academic and project-based growth. He has helped learners build strong foundations, regain confidence, and move forward with clarity, including learners who have gone on to attend UC Berkeley, Occidental College, Boston University, and Tufts University.

His approach is relationship driven and grounded in trust. Albert supports learners beyond coursework by helping them develop study habits, organization, and ownership of their progress, while keeping parents informed and confident that things are on track.

Albert holds a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering from the University of Colorado Boulder and has worked in education technology at Khan Academy and Guild Education.
