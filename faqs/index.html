Frequently Asked Questions

A few of the questions families ask most. If yours is not here, reach out anytime.

What services do you offer?

I specialize in one-on-one work in project-based mentorship, math for grades 6 through 12, and Mandarin Chinese.

What is your tutoring approach?

My personalized approach partners with each learner to build confidence, work through academic challenges, and reach their goals.

What ages of learners do you work with?

I work with learners in middle school and high school.

How are sessions structured?

I offer one-on-one sessions tailored to each learner's specific needs.

How much is each session?

Pricing varies by grade level, the learner's needs, and the length of the engagement. Project-based mentorship uses lump-sum pricing.

When are sessions held?

I currently schedule learners on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday evenings, Pacific Time.

How do I schedule a session?

Reach out through the contact page to set up an intake call where we can talk through goals.

What results can I expect?

The focus is on deep understanding, lasting study habits, and skills that extend well beyond our sessions.

Do you offer online or in-person sessions?

Online sessions are preferred, as they offer greater flexibility and convenience for everyone.

Still have a question?

The best next step is a short intake call. Tell me about your learner and I will reach out.
